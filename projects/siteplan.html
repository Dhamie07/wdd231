Chocolate Connoisseur - Site Plan

Site Name

Chocolate Connoisseur - A website dedicated to chocolate appreciation, tasting, and education. This name reflects the site's goal of guiding users through the world of artisan chocolates.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to educate visitors about chocolate, including tasting techniques, pairings, artisan brands, and recipes. It serves as a hub for chocolate lovers to deepen their knowledge and appreciation.

Scenarios

  • What are the best ways to taste and evaluate chocolate like an expert?
  • Where can I find high-quality, ethically sourced chocolate brands?

Color Schema

Dark Brown (#4E342E) - Used for headers and accents, representing rich chocolate tones.

Cream (#FFF3E0) - Used for background and main text areas, providing a warm, inviting feel.

Typography

Header Font: Playfair Display - Used for headings to create an elegant look.

Body Font: Open Sans - Used for readability in paragraphs.
